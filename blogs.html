Three Eras Of Car Technology

The Early Era (1886-1945)

This era saw the invention of the internal combustion engine and the birth of the automobile, followed by Henry Ford's revolutionary assembly line that made cars accessible to the masses.

The Modern Era (1945-2010)

This era witnessed significant advancements in comfort and safety features, with innovations like power steering, air conditioning, and anti-lock brakes becoming commonplace.

The Digital Era (2010-Present)

This era is characterized by connectivity, autonomous driving technologies, and the rise of electric vehicles, transforming cars into intelligent and connected machines.
